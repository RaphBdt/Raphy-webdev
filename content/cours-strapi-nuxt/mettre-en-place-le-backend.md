## Why Strapi?

The advantage of Strapi compared to other CMS (WordPress, Prestashop...) is that it allows you to create controllers in order to change the native behavior of the backend. It is therefore completely adjustable according to your needs. In addition, Strapi natively creates an API, so the frontend is completely decoupled from the backend.
